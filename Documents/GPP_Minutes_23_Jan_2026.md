### **2. Theme & Concept Confirmation**

The group agreed to work under the theme _Connected Places: Connected Ambient Devices Across the Miles (Kilometres)_.

We proposed an IoT concept combining **sport and healthcare**, where two users in different locations can:

- Track activity data (e.g. punch count, physical performance)

- Enter a competitive mode

- View each other’s status through connected ambient devices

The concept follows the principles of _glanceable_ design and _pre-attentive processing_, aiming to minimise mental effort.
